Privacy Policy

Screenshot & Image Editor Chrome Extension

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Overview

Scruman ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a screenshot capture and image editing Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our extension.

âœ… Scruman does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Screenshots or images you capture
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Location data
  • Any other user data

How the Extension Works

When you use Scruman:

  • Screenshot capture: Images are captured locally in your browser and never leave your device
  • Image editing: All editing happens locally in your browser
  • Temporary storage: Screenshots are temporarily stored in browser memory only during the editing session
  • Export: When you download or copy an image, it is saved directly to your device or clipboard

All operations are performed entirely on your local device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions Used

Scruman requires the following Chrome permissions to function:

  • activeTab: To capture screenshots of the current tab when you click the extension
  • scripting: To enable the "Select Area" feature and handle full-page captures
  • storage: To temporarily store screenshot data between capture and editing (local only)

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and never for data collection.

Third-Party Services

Scruman does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. The extension operates completely offline after installation.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no user data to secure on our end. All your screenshots and edited images remain on your local device under your control.

Children's Privacy

Scruman does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all screenshots and images you create with Scruman - they exist only on your device.
